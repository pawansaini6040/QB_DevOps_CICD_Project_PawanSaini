About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Engineer – Product Delivery to join Appnomic as a technical specialist working with our strategic clients. You will be responsible for implementing, configuring, and optimizing AppsOne solutions to monitor client applications and infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities

Develop a deep understanding of the AppsOne solution suite and its capabilities through training and hands-on experience
Understand and document client application landscape and infrastructure
Gather details of known application and infrastructure outages and pain points
Understand customers' requirements and translate them into specific monitoring requirements
Review implementation plans and obtain sign-off from client project managers
Perform hands-on implementation and configuration of AppsOne to monitor client applications and infrastructure
Ensure on-time delivery of tasks and completion of AppsOne implementation milestones
Analyze alerts and issues identified by AppsOne to prepare detailed Root Cause Analysis reports
Fine-tune AppsOne configuration based on analysis of alerts
Provide insights to clients on infrastructure changes and enhancements based on AppsOne analysis
Train clients and Appnomic monitoring teams on AppsOne features and proactive monitoring capabilities
Document best practices for deploying AppsOne in customer environments
Track support requirements and interface with AppsOne support team for quick resolutions
Manage AppsOne patch updates and upgrades on an ongoing basis
Ensure complete deployment documentation of AppsOne in client environments
Continuously monitor and improve deployments
Troubleshoot configuration, environment, and application server issues
Lead regular meetings with clients and internal stakeholders
Provide feedback to engineering and product management teams for product improvements
